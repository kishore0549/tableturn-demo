Design System Notes
Reference points for building in Figma or Framer
🎨
Colour Palette
Midnight navy base (#0A0A14) with electric lime accent (#C8F04D). Coral for alerts (#FF5C4D), violet for upsell moments (#7B5CF5). High contrast, maximum pop.
#C8F04D Lime#7B5CF5 Violet#FF5C4D Coral
✍️
Typography
Syne for headings — chunky, editorial, modern. DM Sans for body — clean and highly legible at small sizes. Both free on Google Fonts.
Syne 800 — HeadersDM Sans 400/600 — Body
⚡
The Upsell Moment
Screen 3 is your sales pitch in one card. Violet border, "87% also ordered" social proof badge, single tap to add. This is the feature that closes deals — make sure it lands perfectly in demos.
Social proof framingOne-tap add
📐
Layout Rules
18--20px border radius on cards. 16px horizontal gutters. Lime CTA buttons always full-width at bottom. Dark cards on dark background — border creates separation.
18px radius16px gutters
💚
Gen-Z Design Cues
No white backgrounds. Dark mode native. Emoji used functionally. Radial gradient hero images. Glassmorphism badges. Bold weight contrast between 300 and 800. Feels like Spotify meets Deliveroo.
Dark nativeBold contrastGradients
